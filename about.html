About Me

I am a passionate Computer Science Engineering student at UPES, with a keen interest in software development , cloud computing , and web technologies . My focus lies in building scalable, innovative applications while exploring cloud infrastructures and virtualization technologies.

Skilled in Java, C, and Python, I enjoy creating secure, efficient solutions across the full development stack . From web development to system administration and database management , I am driven by a desire to continuously learn and improve .

Outside of academics, I serve as a Public Relations Core Committee Member at Cyber Sentinel UPES , where I collaborate with peers to organize tech events and foster a spirit of innovation . These experiences have helped me grow as a leader and problem-solver .

Ultimately, I aim to use technology to make a positive impact , continually pushing myself to explore new possibilities and contribute to the ever-evolving world of tech .
